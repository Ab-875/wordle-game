Wordle

How to Play

Try to Guess the Unknown word in 6 attempts

The color of the boxes will change to show how close your guess was to the word

  • Green: The letter is correct and is in the right position

  • Yellow: The letter is correct but is not in the correct position

  • Gray: The letter is not present

Press reset to play again with a different target word
